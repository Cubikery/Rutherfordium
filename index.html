Our objective for this project was to design, develop and manufacture the fastest miniature F1 car at the Saint Kentigern College STEM Racing in Schools competition,
>in addition to creating the best presented portfolios and trade display.
achieve this goal, we must incorporate innovation in both design and engineering, while also meeting all the technical criteria outlined in the guidelines.
Specific - design and manufacture a STEM Racing car that meets the regulations and has a race time of xx seconds
Measureable - secure at least $1,500 in sponsorships
Achievable - Use clear team roles, weekly planning meetings, and expert feedback to meet all technical and documentation requirements within project deadlines.
Relevant - Enhancing our STEM design and teamwork skills through this SKC comp.
Time-bound - complete testing, documents and presentations by the 13th of June.
